Pizza

Description

A delicious fusion of Italian and Asian flavours. This pizza uses a sweet and savoury hoisin sauce base, topped with tender chicken and fresh vegetables.

Ingredients

  • 1 pre-made pizza base
  • 4 tbsp hoisin sauce
  • 150g cooked chicken, shredded
  • 1/2 red bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 3 spring onions, chopped
  • 150g mozzarella cheese, grated
  • 1 tsp sesame seeds (optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 220C/200C Fan/Gas 7.
  2. Place the pizza base on a baking tray. Spread the hoisin sauce evenly over the base, leaving a small border around the edge.
  3. Sprinkle half of the mozzarella cheese over the sauce.
  4. Arrange the shredded chicken and sliced red pepper over the cheese.
  5. Top with the remaining mozzarella cheese and half of the chopped spring onions.
  6. Bake for 10-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  7. Remove from the oven, garnish with the remaining spring onions and a sprinkle of sesame seeds if using. Slice and serve immediately.
