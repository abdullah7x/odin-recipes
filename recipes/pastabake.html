Pasta Bake

Description

A classic comfort food, this easy and cheesy pasta bake is perfect for a weeknight family dinner. It's simple to make and always a crowd-pleaser.

Ingredients

  • 400g dried pasta (penne or fusilli)
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 500g passata
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 200g cheddar cheese, grated
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 200C/180C Fan/Gas 6.
  2. Cook the pasta according to packet instructions, until it is slightly undercooked. Drain and set aside.
  3. While the pasta cooks, heat the olive oil in a large pan. Add the onion and cook gently for 5-8 minutes until softened. Add the garlic and cook for another minute.
  4. Pour in the passata, add the oregano, and season with salt and pepper. Bring to a simmer and cook for 5 minutes.
  5. Add the drained pasta to the sauce and stir until everything is well coated.
  6. Pour the pasta and sauce mixture into an ovenproof dish. Sprinkle the grated cheese evenly over the top.
  7. Bake for 20-25 minutes, or until the top is golden and bubbling.
